About this page

This page represents my work completed on programming assigments, code, and demos during my CSC 344 Programming Languages course at SUNY Oswego. Some of the work represents written assigments on problem sets, including language description exercises as well as assorted essays pertaining to programming languages. All of the work is presented chronologically with due dates and includes links to the assignemnts specifications as well as solutions as indicated by the hyperlinks. The web page is designed to open the pdfs in your browser however your exact browser specifications may may make you download the respective documents.
